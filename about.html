About Us

Extrusion Works Limited is a Company wholly owned by Zambians. It was incorporated Under the Registration of Companies and Business Names Act CAP 389 of the Laws of Zambia as a Construction company.

Vision

Extrusion Works Limited vision is to grow into internationally recognised Building maintenance, Aluminium, ceilings and glazing supply and installations specialists best known for quality providing the best technical support to customers in Africa and all over the world.
